Please note that you cannot edit an email template once it has been sent out to participants.
These are the tags that you can use within the email templates:
  • <FIRSTNAME> - The participant's first name.
  • <FULLNAME> - The participant's full name.
  • <UID> - The participant's university ID.
  • <LANDING_LISTENER> - URL to the 'landing page' listener.
  • <ISIS_LISTENER> - URL to the fake ISIS page we use.
